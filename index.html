Our History

At Flavours of Tandoor, our philosophy revolves around the fusion of tradition and innovation. We deeply respect the time-honoured traditions of Indian cuisine, while embracing the excitement of modern flavours. Our commitment to quality ingredients, inclusive hospitality, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere forms the foundation of our culinary journey. We see our restaurant as a place where people come together, share meals, and create lasting connections, and we aim to offer our guests a memorable culinary adventure that reflects the diverse and vibrant essence of India.
